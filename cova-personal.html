Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common personal insurance questions for phones, cars, health and travel.

Can I claim for a stolen phone?
Yes. If your phone is stolen and it was insured under your electronic equipment policy, Cova helps you submit the claim and track settlement through WhatsApp.
What happens after a motor accident?
Once you report the accident, your handler guides you through document submission, insurer assessment and repairs until your claim is closed.
Do I need a medical exam for life insurance?
It depends on the sum assured and product, but Cova will tell you upfront whether a medical exam is needed and help you schedule it.
Which hospitals are covered by health plans?
Cova works with insurers that cover a wide hospital network in Nigeria; your handler will share the list that applies to your selected plan.
Can I use Cova travel insurance for a Schengen visa?
Yes. Our travel policies include Schengen-approved medical cover options for visa applications and international emergencies.
