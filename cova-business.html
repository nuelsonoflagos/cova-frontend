Business insurance FAQ

Common SME questions about group cover, compliance and asset protection.

How do I enroll employees in group health?
We gather employee data once, then issue a group plan with a single renewal date. Your handler helps you add staff and confirm eligible hospitals.
Does workmen's compensation meet NSITF requirements?
Yes. Our workmen's compensation plans satisfy NSITF guidelines and help your business comply with labour regulations.
How is a fleet policy managed?
Fleet cover is issued to the company, and we manage vehicle additions, renewals and claims through one account with your Cova handler.
What is the indemnity period for consequential loss?
Consequential loss typically covers business interruption for a fixed indemnity period, usually 12 months from the date of an insured event.
Do I need a staff list for fidelity guarantee?
Yes. Fidelity guarantee requires a list of covered employees so the policy can protect against dishonest acts by named staff members.
